Martin Jawahier


My name is Martin Jawahier. I'm a 20 years old artist studying Game Design at the Hanze University of Applied Sciences in Groningen, The Netherlands.

I enjoy evoking emotions through art. While speech can be used to tell stories, the story always feels related to the person telling it. I believe that if one tries to tell stories through art, it evokes personal emotions rather than sympathy for the story teller. That is exactly what I want to achieve.

Masks

This symbolic piece sheds light on how people act amongst oneanother, in person as well as on social media.

The person in the drawing is controlled like a puppet. Whenever the person faces social encounters, he is forced by social pressure to put on a mask, hiding his real emotions.

Running away from the masks proves to be no easy task, as the person is chained to the malicious puppet master, his own demons.

Swamp Cottage

Swamp Cottage is a small hut in the middle of a swamp.

The purpose of this drawing was to practice reflections, rivers, ground decoration, trees, wood textures, and light.
